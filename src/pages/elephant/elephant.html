FACT ABOUT ELEPHANT

Elephants are the largest land animals.  Elephants are very social animals and live in family groups referred to as "herds". Female elephants are also called "cows" and males are called "bulls".  Elephants have a 22 month gestation (pregnancy) and produce one offspring at a time. The baby is called a "calf".They have characteristic long noses, or trunks; large, floppy ears; and wide, thick legs. There are two species of elephant. The Asian elephant and the African elephant live on separate continents and have many unique features
